The classes and its usage is given below.
1. white-heading                    - displays the heading in white color.
2. website-banner-title-size        - displays the title in bigger size.
3. website-banner-name-middle       - displays the title in middle of the webpage.
4. website-banner-name-middle-right - displays the title in middle right of webpage.
5. website-banner-name-middle-left  - displays the title in middle left of webpage.
6. banner-title-brief-size          - displays content below the title in medium size.
7. banner-title-brief-middle        - displays below the middle banner title.
8. banner-title-brief-right         - displays below the right middle banner title.
9. banner-title-brief-left          - displays below the left middle banner title.
10. header-left			- displays the heading in leftside.
11. header-right			- displays the heading in rightside.
12. header-center			- displays the heading in center.
13. header-explanation			- used below the header classes for the neat and clear view.
14. darkblue-background		- displays with background of darkblue.
15. violet-background			- displays with background of violet.
16. lightgrey-background		- displays with background of light grey.
17. form-field				- creates the form field very attractive.
18. form-field-group			- displays the group for form fields with correct height.
19. input:focus			- displays the particular field wiht shadow at back when clicking.
20. center-text			- displays the text in center.
21. blue-button			- displays the button in blue color.
22. coding-box				- used particularly to display code.
23. content-box			- used to display a content inside a box.
24. shadow				- displays the shadow for element.
25. paragraph				- it is mainly used to display a paragraph neatly.
